Maddox

Bierman

An incoming freshman at the University of Memphis pursuing a BFA in Performance/Acting, Maddox has been captivating audiences since the age of seven. His versatility shines through his performances in principal roles, ensemble work, and featured dance numbers.

His theatrical journey spans productions with New Day Children's Theatre, Collierville High School, West Collierville Middle School, and the Collierville Arts Council, even performing at the Orpheum Theatre Memphis. Beyond performing, Maddox dedicates his time to mentoring young talent at New Day Children's Theatre, sharing his passion and inspiring the next generation of performers.
